# Formatting Rules

## Tone
- concise
- calm
- no hype
- no long setup paragraph

## Bullet construction
Each bullet should ideally contain:
- what happened
- why it matters in a few words

Good pattern:
- `Company X did Y — matters because Z.`

## Section limits
- Top Stories: 3-4 bullets preferred, 5 max
- Interesting Threads: 0-2 preferred, 3 max
- Tech News: 2-3 preferred, 4 max
- Local News: 2-3 preferred, 4 max
- Action Items: 0-2 preferred

## Action Items rule
Include Action Items only when there is a concrete thing Daneek may want to do, verify, watch, or revisit soon.

## Source diversity rule
Try to avoid having more than half the total bullets come from a single outlet unless the news cycle truly warrants it.

## Token discipline
Do not over-research.
Stop once you have enough material to fill the briefing well.
Prefer a smaller set of high-signal sources over many shallow reads.
